ABOUT CREATOR

Stefanie Tam is an LA based multidisciplinary designer and artist. Her current personal practice, foundationally grounded by design and often pertaining to the indexical and archival, ranges from directing printed publications, coding funky web toys, to curating books as art objects. While utilizing traditional design mediums as tools to execute her work, her practice oftentimes blurs the already loose definitions of and distinctions between art and design.
